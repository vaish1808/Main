DEMAT Account Services

Account Features

  • Secure storage of financial securities in electronic form.
  • Convenient online access to view holdings and transactions.
  • Quick transfer of shares between Demat accounts.
  • Faster settlement of trades compared to physical delivery.

Opening a DEMAT Account

To open a DEMAT account, follow these steps:

  1. Visit our nearest branch or website.
  2. Submit KYC documents including identity proof, address proof, and PAN card.
  3. Complete the account opening form provided by our Depository Participant (DP).

Charges Associated

  • Account Opening Charges: Many DPs charge a one-time fee for opening a Demat account.
  • Annual Maintenance Charges (AMC): DPs may charge an annual fee to maintain the Demat account.
  • Transaction Charges: Charges are applied for buying and selling securities.
